APPLIED ENGINEERING...

Is a field of study that works with the use, creation and components of a new/old product. It combines both engineering and bussiness to help solve some of the most complex problems in our world.

My game features an alien character that is running around trying to get all the coins. If you win you move on to the next level but if you fail (by running into the spikes or the water) you die and the game restarts. If you beat level one, watch out for level two.
