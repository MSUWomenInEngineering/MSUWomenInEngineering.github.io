After attending Women in Engineering Summer Camp at MSU, I have decided that my primary interest in engineering is in computer science. I enjoy computer science because I can build programs (like games or websites) almost from scratch. I also enjoy computer science because it involves logical thinking and problem solving, like puzzles. I hope to continue programming on a small scale and exploring my interest in this area.

This is one of the projects I completed this week- my game. It features a cuddly-looking alien character exploring a planet with an orange sky and red grass. If you collect all of the coins, you win! Once you have won, you can choose to restart the game or to ignore the window. There are also two large walls to protect the alien man from falling off the edge of the world.
